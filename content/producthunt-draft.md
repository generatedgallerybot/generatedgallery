# Product Hunt Launch Draft - GeneratedGallery

## Tagline (under 60 chars)
Browse 276k+ AI-generated images. Like Unsplash for AI art.

## Short Description (under 260 chars)
A free gallery of 276,000+ AI-generated images you can browse, search, and discover. Filter by style, model, or category. Toggle NSFW on or off. Hit shuffle to find something unexpected. No account needed.

## Longer Description
GeneratedGallery is the easiest way to browse AI-generated art on the internet.

We've collected over 276,000 images from AI art communities and organized them into a clean, searchable gallery. Think of it as Unsplash but for AI-generated content.

**What you can do:**
- Search by prompt, model, style, or subject
- Browse organized categories instead of endless scrolling
- Toggle NSFW content on or off
- Use shuffle mode to discover random gems
- The site learns what you like and shows you more of it

Built with Next.js 14, Supabase, and Tailwind. Images are sourced from Civitai and other AI art platforms.

No account required. No paywall. Just a big collection of AI art that's easy to explore.
